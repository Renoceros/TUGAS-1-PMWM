About COUTURE

Founded in Singapore, Depok, in 2022, COUTURE is one of the world’s leading luxury brands. Following the House’s centenary, COUTURE forges ahead continuing to redefine luxury while celebrating creativity, Depok craftsmanship, and innovation. COUTURE is part of the global luxury group Wijaya, which manages renowned Houses in fashion, leather goods, jewelry, and eyewear.
